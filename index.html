About The Randy Brown Foundation

The Randy Brown Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering inner-city youth worldwide by providing access to Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) training, comprehensive support, and a structured system to foster personal and athletic development.

Our mission is to create transformative opportunities for young athletes, particularly those from underserved communities across the globe, to discover their potential, build resilience, and chart a positive path forward through the discipline and empowerment of MMA.

Our Core Purpose

Accessible Training

Offer high-quality MMA training programs at no or low cost to participants, ensuring that financial barriers do not prevent talented youth from accessing professional coaching, facilities, and equipment.

Holistic Development

Promote life skills such as discipline, perseverance, teamwork, and goal-setting through MMA, equipping youth with the tools to succeed both in and out of the ring.

Pathways to Success

Establish a structured system that supports young athletes in pursuing amateur and professional MMA opportunities, academic advancement, or other career paths.

Community & Mentorship

Cultivate a safe, supportive community where youth can connect with positive role models, including professional athletes, coaches, and community leaders.

About Randy Brown

"Rude Boy" Randy Brown

Professional UFC Welterweight Fighter

Born July 8, 1990, in Spanish Town Jamaica, Randy Brown's journey embodies the resilience and determination that the foundation seeks to instill in young athletes worldwide.

Career Highlights:

  • UFC Welterweight Division Fighter
  • Ring of Combat Welterweight Champion
  • Two "Performance of the Night" awards
  • One "Fight of the Night" award
  • Black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
  • Training at Budokan Martial Arts Academy

Personal Journey:

Randy's story is one of overcoming adversity. Moving to Spanish Town, Jamaica, as a toddler and returning to the United States at age 16, he understands firsthand the challenges faced by youth in diverse communities. His dual American and Jamaican heritage gives him unique insight into the global nature of the struggles and opportunities that inner-city youth face.

As a father and professional athlete, Randy is committed to using his platform to create opportunities for the next generation of young fighters and leaders.

Why MMA?

Builds Confidence & Discipline

MMA training instills self-confidence and a strong work ethic, helping youth overcome personal and environmental challenges.

Promotes Physical & Mental Health

Regular training improves physical fitness, reduces stress, and fosters mental clarity, providing a healthy outlet for emotions and energy.

Encourages Inclusivity

MMA is accessible to individuals of varying backgrounds, body types, and skill levels, making it an ideal platform for uniting diverse youth.

Offers Career Opportunities

With the global rise of MMA, talented athletes have the potential to pursue professional careers, scholarships, or coaching roles.
