Our Mission

When Hurricane Melissa struck Jamaica, countless families were left with damaged homes and limited access to essentials. The Randy Brown Foundation is mobilizing support affected communities through direct aid, construction materials, and volunteer service.

Our goal is simple — to provide immediate relief and long-term support that helps families rebuild and recover stronger than before.

Our Impact

$50,000.00 USD GOING TOWARDS RELIEF

We're focusing on the communities most affected by Hurricane Melissa, with efforts centered in:

Westmoreland, St. Elizabeth and St. Catherine.

Projected Reach:

  • 300+ families receiving care packages
  • 50+ homes supported with rebuilding materials
  • 25+ volunteers mobilized on the ground
